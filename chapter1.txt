Chapter 1: The Whispering Forest 🌿

At the edge of a quiet village stood a vast forest that many people avoided. The villagers said the forest was alive—not in the ordinary way of birds and animals, but in a deeper, mysterious way. The wind seemed to whisper through the tall trees, and the rivers hummed soft melodies as they flowed over smooth stones.
One afternoon, a curious young boy named Daniel decided to walk into the forest. He had always loved nature—the smell of wet soil after rain, the bright green leaves that danced in sunlight, and the gentle rustle of grass in the wind.
As Daniel walked deeper into the forest, he noticed something strange. The trees seemed taller than usual, and the air felt fresh and powerful, as if the forest itself was breathing.
Suddenly, a soft voice echoed through the leaves.

“Welcome, Daniel.”

Daniel froze.

He looked around, but no one was there.
Only the trees, the wind, and the endless green world around him.
