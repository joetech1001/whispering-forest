Chapter 2: The Guardian of the River 🌊

Daniel followed the sound of flowing water until he reached a beautiful river hidden deep in the forest. The water sparkled under the sunlight, and colorful birds rested on the branches above it.
As he stepped closer, the water began to swirl gently. Slowly, a glowing figure rose from the river—a woman made of light and flowing water.

“I am the Guardian of Nature,” she said softly.

Daniel’s eyes widened with amazement.

“For centuries,” she continued, “this forest has protected life. The trees clean the air, the rivers give water, and the animals keep the balance of nature. But humans are beginning to forget this.”
Daniel looked around the forest with new understanding. Every leaf, every drop of water, every creature had a purpose.

“What can I do?” he asked quietly.

The guardian smiled.

“Remember what you see today—and teach others to respect nature.”
