Chapter 3: The Promise 🌳

As the sun began to set, the forest glowed with golden light. Daniel walked back toward the village, but something inside him had changed.
He no longer saw the forest as just trees and rivers. He saw it as a living world full of wisdom and balance.
From that day forward, Daniel shared the story of the whispering forest with everyone he met. He planted trees, protected the rivers, and encouraged others to care for the earth.

Years later, the forest still stood tall, greener and stronger than ever.

But one evening, as Daniel stood at the edge of the forest, the wind carried a whisper once again.

This time, the voice sounded different—soft, but worried.

“Daniel… we need your help.”
